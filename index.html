Our Story

Founded in 2015, Yamba Media began as a small creative studio with a big vision: to help businesses tell their stories through compelling visual media. What started with six friends and a camera has grown into a full-service media agency with a team of talented professionals.

We believe in the power of storytelling to connect brands with their audiences. Our approach combines creative vision with strategic thinking to deliver results that matter.

"Our mission is to create media that not only looks great but also drives meaningful engagement and delivers measurable results for our clients."
- Sidney Muyamba, CEO & Founder
